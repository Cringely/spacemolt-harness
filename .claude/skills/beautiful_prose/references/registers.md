# Registers

## literary_modern (default): aggressive entropy
Vivid, lean imagery. Controlled heat, sharp observation. Minimal ornament.
Entropy: fragments allowed. Conjunction openers allowed. Chaotic length variation encouraged; single-word sentences legal as impact.

## cold_steel: aggressive entropy
Severe compression. Punchy, unsentimental. High signal, low warmth.
Entropy: same allowances as literary_modern, compressed harder.

## founding_fathers: rhythm variation only
Formal, spare, civic gravity. Balanced syntax without decoration. Moral clarity without sermon.
Entropy: vary sentence length and openers; no fragments, no conjunction openers. This is the register for policy documents, standards, and governance prose.

## journalistic: rhythm variation only
Crisp, factual, narrative clarity. Clean momentum, no clickbait cadence.
Entropy: vary length and structure; no fragments. This is the register for reports, summaries, and status prose.

## Examples

Bad: "This isn't about money. It's about power."
Good: "Money is the instrument. Power is the habit."

Bad: "At its core, this is a complex issue. That said, in today's world..."
Good: "It is complex. Complexity is not an excuse for fog."
